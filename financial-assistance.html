Financial Assistance

Enrol immediatley with no upfront costs

The total cost of your Diploma can be placed through *VET FEE-HELP provided you are and Australian Citizen.

What is VET FEE-HELP?

VET FEE-HELP is an Australian Government loan scheme that helps eligible students pay their tuition fees for diploma courses undertaken at an approved provider (Didasko Institute of Business, through our partner NMIT, is approved by the Australian Government to offer VET FEE-HELP loans to eligible students).

Am I eligible for VET FEE-HELP?

You may be eligible for a VET FEE-HELP loan if you:

  • are an Australian citizen or permanent humanitarian visa holder resident in Australia.
  • are studying a VET accredited diploma, advanced diploma, graduate certificate or graduate diploma at an approved provider.

How do I apply?

We will arrange the processing of your VET FEE-HELP application. You will need to provide your tax file number to complete the application.

How much can I borrow?

There is a set maximum amount you can borrow through VET FEE-HELP over your lifetime. This is known as the FEE-HELP limit. In 2014 the general FEE-HELP limit is $96,000.

How do I repay the loan?

You only commence repaying this Government Loan once you are in full-time employment and earning above $51,309 (2014 threshold). Once these two criteria are met, you make a small payment to reduce the loan each week from withheld tax via your weekly pay.

When can I start?

You can start your Business Diploma course as soon as you have been offered a place. We have rolling weekly course intakes every Monday, so there is no waiting for next year or mid-year intakes. Your course begins when you want to begin.

*Eligibility criteria applies.
